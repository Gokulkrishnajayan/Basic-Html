THRISSUR

Thrissur is a city in the south Indian state of Kerala. It's known for sacred sites and colorful fesivals. In the center is Vadakkunnathan Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva and adorned with murals

The Top attractions to visit in Thrissur District is :

  • Athirapally Waterfalls
  • Guruvayur Temple
  • Kauthuka Park
  • Snow Storm
